About COSMO

The COSMO group is part of the Brazilian Center for Physics Research (CBPF), located in Rio de Janeiro. Our team conducts research across a wide range of topics in cosmology, astrophysics, and fundamental interactions, combining theoretical, observational, and computational approaches. We are also part os many experimental and observational collaborations.

We work at the intersection of research development, training of human resources, education, and outreach, focusing on delivering high-impact results and strengthening Brazil’s presence in cutting-edge international projects.
